OPEN DOORS

Visit any participating space, see what they are about and meet the people who call it home. At least one day is free in every space.

EVENTS

Participate in workshops, meet-ups and exhibitions in the spaces. Details in the schedule below.

TOURS

Learn more about a space through an organized tour. There are guided tours for single and multiple locations. See TOURS for details.
